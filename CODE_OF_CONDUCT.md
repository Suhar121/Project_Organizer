# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, collaborative, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and provide helpful feedback.
- Focus on ideas, not individuals.
- Welcome newcomers and different perspectives.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination.
- Abusive, hateful, or threatening language.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community interactions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security/advisory channel if available, or contact the maintainers through repository contacts.
